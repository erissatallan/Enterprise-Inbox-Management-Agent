I just shipped a new applied AI project:

Enterprise Inbox-to-Workflow Agent

Problem:
Ops and support teams still spend too much time manually triaging inbound requests across email/chat, then manually routing actions. That slows response times and weakens consistency.

What I built:
A policy-gated AI workflow agent that
- classifies intent and priority,
- proposes structured actions,
- auto-executes safe cases,
- routes risky/uncertain cases to human review,
- logs full decision/action traces for auditability.

Baseline metrics (validation set):
- Intent accuracy: 91.67%
- Priority accuracy: 100%
- Action correctness: 100%
- Policy accuracy: 91.67%
- Auto-execute rate: 41.67%
- Review rate: 58.33%

This is the direction I am pursuing in applied AI: measurable operations value with controlled autonomy, not black-box automation.
